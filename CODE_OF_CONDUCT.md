# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our
community a welcoming and respectful experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Public or private harassment of any kind
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of
acceptable behavior. They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code,
issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including issues,
pull requests, discussions, and any other communication channels related to this project.
